About us

A Cliffside Retreat in the Heart of Bohol

Cliff Haven Resort & Restaurant is nestled along Larapan Barangay Road in Jagna, Bohol — offering guests a peaceful escape with breathtaking views, a refreshing pool, and an on-site restaurant serving fresh Filipino and international cuisine.

Whether you're here for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a quick stopover, we promise an experience that feels as natural as the landscape around us.
